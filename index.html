Tennis Alberta Programs

Programs run by Tennis Alberta are FREE for participants.

Drop-in is welcome, however we kindly ask that you inform us in advance if you are planning to attend. This helps me prepare the appropriate amount of equipment and court space.

Schedule of programs:

  • 6–8 years old group: Tuesday & Thursday, 4:30–5:45pm
  • 8–11 years old group: Monday, Wednesday, Friday, 4:30–5:45pm
  • 12-17 old group: Monday, Wednesday, Friday, 7:15–8:45am
  • Junior Social Saturday: Saturdays 9:00–11:00am (fitness and match play)
  • Adult lessons: Tuesday & Thursday 7:30–8:45am

Most sessions take place at Lethbridge Tennis Club courts, except for selected Try Tennis events held in community parks. Location details will be provided separately for each event.
